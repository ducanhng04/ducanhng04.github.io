About

The Push-Pull-Legs (PPL) workout plan is a versatile and efficient training routine that divides the body into three main movement patterns: pushing, pulling, and leg exercises. This split allows for balanced muscle group training while providing ample recovery time between sessions. It is ideal for individuals looking to improve overall strength, muscle mass, and endurance. PPL is well-suited for both beginners and advanced lifters, offering flexibility to adjust the frequency and intensity based on fitness goals. Whether you're training for general fitness, bodybuilding, or strength, this structured plan promotes consistent progress and prevents overtraining.


Below is a sample training schedule with specific reps and sets for you to follow along, you can also adjust to suit your condition. Each exercise also has a small video to guide you through the movements, making it easier to access the exercises.
